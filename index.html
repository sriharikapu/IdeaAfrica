Our Story

Africa is a continent with a huge potential, We have a lot of resources and we are among the most youngest population on the planet. The average age of an afriacn is around 20 years. This implies that we have more human resources. The power to empower the people of the countries of africa lies in the education of the younger generation. We dont want our kids and childern to be struggling for surviving. Africa is been a Continent known for its sustainability. We are proud of our history, and future lies in the actions that we take now.

It is our vision to build a community where the less priviledged can dream again and be empowered to see their dreams come true.

Mission

Idea Africa aims to change the game for thousands of young Africans as it supports them to realize their potentials and create opportunities for themselves through the implementation of innovation ideas with focus on Quality education
